About

The College of IST at Penn State empowers students to become technology professionals, leaders, and innovators. We offer interdisciplinary programs focused on data science, cybersecurity, enterprise technology integration, and more. With strong ties to industry and a commitment to hands-on learning, our graduates leave prepared to solve complex global challenges.

Services

  • Advising: Personalized academic guidance to help you stay on track.
  • Career Solutions: Resume reviews, career fairs, internships, and employer connections.
  • Learning Spaces: Access to state-of-the-art labs and research centers.
  • Tutoring: Academic support from peer mentors.
